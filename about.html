About Me

I'm a gameplay programmer with a focus on systems design, predictable multiplayer behavior, and clean, testable code. I prefer to prototype rapidly and iterate based on playtesting and feel.

Technical Skills

  • Languages: C#, C++, Blueprints
  • Engines: Unity (DOTS / Netcode), Unreal Engine 4/5
  • Multiplayer: Client-server authority models, rollback & prediction concepts
  • Tools: Git, Perforce, custom editor tooling, Unity Editor scripting

Selected Experience

I have built prototypes and systems for multiplayer shooters, AR interactive experiences, and extensible gameplay frameworks. I enjoy working in cross-disciplinary teams where design and engineering iterate closely.

Approach & Process

I prototype systems to validate the feel as early as possible. For multiplayer projects I:

  1. Define authority model and data ownership.
  2. Prototype a minimal interaction set to test latency/prediction assumptions.
  3. Iterate features with automated tests and playtests.


My goal is systems that communicate clearly to players and are affordable to maintain by teams of 1-10 people.
